Checklist

Vendor review playbook

A clear checklist helps teams avoid missed steps. Later in this course, we will turn similar workflow ideas into JavaScript, React, backend, database, and AI-assisted features.

Review steps

  1. Confirm business owner and vendor purpose.
  2. Collect security questionnaire or equivalent evidence.
  3. Review data access and system integration scope.
  4. Assign risk level and required follow-up actions.
  5. Record reviewer decision and approval date.
